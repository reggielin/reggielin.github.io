REGINALD LIN
VISUAL COMMUNICATION
The Friendly Sound
Redesign, Distortion

A void space modulating in mysterious ways, / Look at the stars, / We are so far apart,

Norwegian electronic band, Lemaitre, was the focus of a study in which I experimented with how to work with a song's musical qualities and embed that within its typography. Lemaitre's pop-like repetition, use of depth and distance, along with their use of noise and distortions led me towards the visual language that I presented. This project consisted of an album cover and its corresponding process book of fifty potential album covers.
