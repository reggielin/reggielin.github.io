ラジオ体操 (Rajio Taiso)
Movement

morning radio calisthenics:
conveying movement & changing perspectives within a static shot

With the desire to convey movement and to change perspectives within a static shot, ラジオ体操 (Rajio Taiso) was a culmination of these desires. I had the help of eight of my friends to pull off the choreography that I had instructed them to follow, along with the original calisthenics exercises. With the use of physical frames, movement, and sound, I hoped viewers were able to be constantly stimulated with the video’s changing focus.
